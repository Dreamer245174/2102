Data Science and Big Data Technology

Studio of Big Data 2102

Data analysis

Data rules the world, no matter what industry it serves. The demand for efficient use of big data has pushed data science and data analysis tools to the forefront. Data science widely covers statistics, data analysis, data mining and machine learning, and can be used to complex understand and analyze "big data".


Data Science

Data science involves the processing of big data (structured and unstructured), including data preparation, analysis and cleaning. It also involves programming, mathematics, statistics, problem solving, the ability to view things in different ways, intuitively capture data, etc. You can say that data science is a broader term for the technology involved in obtaining insights and information from data.


application area

Big data in communication, retail big data, financial services big data, education big data, digital advertising, Internet search, recommendation system, image / speech recognition, energy management and health care.


Big data trends

The most popular things in big data are talking robots (for real-time support systems – placing orders through text or responses to your transaction queries), accurate product search (providing a better shopping experience in e-commerce sites by accessing user data and providing the best results), and the Internet of things (IOT) (connecting and automating the surrounding world through intelligent networks and response devices) and artificial intelligence (leading major projects with less hardware and more complex clouds).


Integration of data science tradition and future

Following the computer and Internet revolution, big data technology has further swept almost all industries. Under the new mode of "Data-Driven decision-making", many industries and enterprises benefit from big data technology. Geithner consulting, the world's first information technology research and analysis company, predicts that 4.4 million big data related jobs will be added worldwide next year, and 25% of institutions will set up chief data officer positions. Ge is starting to invest $1.5 billion in the San Francisco Bay area to establish a global software and Analysis Center. In addition, according to CSDN (China Software Development Alliance) 2016 China big data survey report, 32.5% of companies across the country are building big data platform, 29.5% of companies have practiced big data in production environment and have successful application cases and products, and 24.5% of companies have made enough understanding and are ready for development.In the talent market, the social demand for big data related posts is also unprecedented. The big data talent market salary report released by CSDN website shows that there is an increasing demand for big data related positions in the society, and the salary is correspondingly higher than that of general it positions. The cities with the strongest demand for big data jobs are Beijing, Shanghai and Shenzhen. At present, there are about 100000 job vacancies. The Internet, real estate and financial industries have the strongest demand, ranking among the top three. Relevant people predict that the talent demand gap in the domestic big data field will reach 1.5 million in the next 3-5 years.
