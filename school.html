Studio of Big Data 2102

The University

Xi’an University of Science and Technology selected in the education and training plan for outstanding engineers of the Ministry of education, the universities implementing the basic capacity-building project of colleges and universities in central and Western China, the universities implementing the national innovation and entrepreneurship training plan for college students, the universities implementing the national new engineering research and practice project, the excellent schools evaluating the undergraduate teaching level of the Ministry of education, the high-level universities of Shaanxi Province and Shaanxi Province As a member of CDIO Engineering Education Alliance, it is one of the first batch of pilot schools for the construction of "first-class universities and first-class disciplines" and the school of emergency management, and one of the founders of the "1 + 2 + 1 China US talent training plan".

Faculty

As of October 2019, the university has nearly 2300 teaching staff, more than 1300 full-time teachers, more than 600 professors and associate professors, and more than 1200 teachers with doctoral and master's degrees. The school also has a high-level part-time teacher team composed of more than 300 experts and professors, including 17 academicians, 1 national teaching team and 28 provincial teaching teams.


Scientific Research Institution

As of October 2019, the university has 18 scientific research platforms at or above the provincial and ministerial level, including the National Key Laboratory of energy and coal quality and clean transformation, the National Key Laboratory of coal industry and mining engineering (provincial and ministerial level), the engineering research center of the Western Ministry of coal mine safety education, and an innovation team of the Ministry of education.


Cooperation and Exchange

By December 2019, it has established cooperative relations with nearly 60 universities and scientific research institutions in more than 20 countries and regions, such as the United States, the United Kingdom, Russia, Japan, the Netherlands and Australia. As one of the founding institutions of the "1 + 2 + 1 China US talent training program", the university has jointly carried out two Sino foreign cooperative school running projects with the University of Tasmania and Macquarie University in Australia.
